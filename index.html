Why MobiCycle Ltd?

The production of modern electronics requires the use of increasingly scarce and expensive resources. Although market demand for electronics is high, society's willingness to repair, reuse or recycle abandoned products is low. As such, discarded electrical and electronic equipment (EEE) represents one the fastest growing waste streams in the world. The ineffective management of EEE leads to higher prices for goods and causes major environmental and health problems. By some estimates, discarded EEE accounts for nearly 70% of the toxic heavy metals found in landfills. Effective management can, however, prevent EEE from going to landfill. MobiCycle's tech solutions support a wide range of interests, from the environmentally conscious family seeking ways to safely discard their electronics, to companies actively engaged in the collection, recycling and treatment of waste. Together, we can safely manage used electronics and electricals for a cleaner, safer & circular planet.
